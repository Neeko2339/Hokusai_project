Thirty-Six Views of Mount Fuji

1829

I shall pour all I have into this to deliver my greatest brush stroke! Bear witness! Thirty-Six Views of Mount Fuji! The Great Wave of Kanagawa... My brush dances!

Series of artful and powerful attacks is unleashed, drawing on the motif of waves and Mount Fuji. The visuals and effects encapsulate Hokusai's artistic style and the essence of his work, blending art and battle in a unique fashion.

A Tour of the Waterfalls of the Provinces

1833

Kirifuri, Kannon, Aoi, Rouben, Yoshitsune, Yourou, Ono, Amida! "A Tour of Waterfalls in Various Provinces"!

innovative depiction of the movement and power of water, combined with Hokusai's attention to detail in the surrounding landscape and figures.
